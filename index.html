About Us

At Kazem Law, we are committed to providing exceptional legal representation and personalized service to our clients. Led by our esteemed attorney, Jonathan Kazem, who brings over 20 years of experience to the practice of law, we have established a reputation for successfully handling a diverse range of legal matters.

Our firm specializes in two primary areas of practice: personal injury claims and contract disputes. We understand the significant impact these legal issues can have on your life, finances, and well-being. Whether you've suffered an injury due to someone else's negligence or are involved in a contract dispute, our team is prepared to fight vigorously on your behalf.

Jonathan Kazem is a seasoned legal professional with an impressive track record. He earned his Juris Doctor from Stetson University College of Law in 1999, and since then, he has been admitted to practice law in various jurisdictions, including the Virginia Bar, Florida Bar, District of Columbia Bar, Pennsylvania Bar, and The United States District Court for the Eastern District of Virginia Bar.

If you have questions about how the Firm can help you with your claim or defense, Kazem Law will be happy to answer your questions and schedule an appointment to meet with you.
