Reckless Driving

Reckless driving charges can carry significant penalties and may impact your license and insurance. Kazem Law helps clients understand the allegations, evaluate the evidence, and build a strategy focused on protecting their record and their future.

How Kazem Law Can Help

We review the circumstances of the stop and the evidence supporting the charge, including speed calculations, radar or lidar use, officer observations, and any video or witness evidence. When appropriate, we work to identify legal and factual issues that can strengthen your defense.

Key Issues We Review

  • Whether the speed or driving behavior was measured accurately
  • Radar/lidar training, calibration, and proper operation
  • Road, traffic, and weather conditions at the time of the stop
  • Negotiation options and alternatives based on the circumstances

What to Expect

We help you prepare for court, explain potential outcomes, and pursue the best available option—whether that involves contesting the charge or negotiating for a more favorable resolution.
