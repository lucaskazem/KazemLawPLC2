Jon is the founder and managing attorney of Kazem Law, PLLC, where he has been practicing personal injury law for more than twenty years. Mr. Kazem has served the Leesburg community for over two decades, and has built a reputation for steady guidance, thorough preparation, and results-driven advocacy for his clients. He received his Juris Doctor from Stetson University College of Law in 1999. He is admitted to the Virginia Bar, Florida Bar, District of Colombia Bar, Pennsylvania Bar, and The United States District Court for the Eastern District of Virginia Bar.

Jon was selected to Super Lawyers for 2022. This peer designation is awarded only to a select number of accomplished attorneys in each state. The Super Lawyers selection process takes into account peer recognition, professional achievement in legal practice, and other cogent factors.

When you are dealing with a serious injury, a complex dispute, or a high-stakes legal problem, you do not just need someone who “knows the law.” You need an attorney who takes the time to understand what happened, explains your options clearly, and then does the work to move your case forward with purpose. Jon’s approach is built around preparation, responsiveness, and practical strategy—so you always know where things stand and what the next step is.

With deep roots in the Leesburg area and more than 20 years of experience, Jon understands the local community and the realities clients face when legal issues disrupt their lives. He focuses on guiding clients through difficult moments with professionalism and compassion, while staying ready to advocate aggressively when the situation calls for it.

Outside of work, Jon is a dedicated family man and the proud father of four kids. He brings the same care, patience, and commitment to his clients that he values at home—treating each case with attention, respect, and a focus on doing what’s right.
